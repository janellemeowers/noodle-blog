Bun Bo Hue, the underrated Vietnamese soup.

Price: $

Everyone loves a bowl of pho, but did you know the range of Vietnamese soups run far and wide?! I'm obsessed with this spicy soup, made with a thicker rice noodle, lemon grass and braised beef. Yum!
